Trump stated this on September the 20th, 2024 in a 2024 campaign ad.
This statement is mostly true.
While running for the Democratic primary in 2019, Vice President Kamala Harris supported access to gender-affirming surgery for individuals in federal prisons and immigration detention. More recently, she has stated that she will "follow the law" on this matter. Federal law mandates that prisons provide necessary medical care, and courts have increasingly ruled that this includes gender-affirming care, even surgery. The Trump administration also acknowledged this legal obligation. However, access to such surgeries has been very limited, with reports showing only two federal inmates having received them, and no documented cases of immigration detainees receiving transgender surgery

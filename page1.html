Kamala stated this on the 7th of October 2024 in an interview.
This statement is false.
Federal data indicates that fentanyl seizures have more than doubled under the Biden-Harris administration. However, this increase in seizures does not necessarily mean that the overall amount of fentanyl entering the U.S. has been reduced. Seizure data only reflects what was intercepted, not the total volume that made it into the country. Experts offer conflicting interpretations: some suggest that increased seizures point to more fentanyl entering the U.S. overall, while others believe the data could support either an increase or a decrease in the drug's flow.
